# Air Astana Brand & Style Guide

This document outlines the brand and style elements for Air Astana, serving as the foundation for the new airline website.

| Element | Specification | Details |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Primary Color** | Deep Blue | #012A48 (Prussian Blue) |
| **Secondary Color** | Golden Hue | #A48C64 (Dark Khaki / Gold) |
| **Accent Color** | Dove Gray | #666666 |
| **Backgrounds** | White / Light Gray | Clean, high-contrast backgrounds for readability. |

## 1. Typography
*   **Primary Font:** Sans-serif (Brand-specific, likely custom or similar to Aeonis Pro Condensed Bold).
*   **Body Font:** Clean, modern sans-serif (e.g., Inter, Roboto, or Open Sans) for high legibility across devices.
*   **Style:** Lowercase styling is often used in the logo for a friendly yet professional appearance. Narrower contours for a modern, efficient look.

## 3. Imagery & Visual Language
*   **Photographic Style:** High-quality, vibrant photography featuring destinations, modern aircraft interiors, and hospitable crew members.
*   **Themes:** National pride, luxury, reliability, and smooth travel experiences.
*   **Banners:** Rich, full-width banners with clear call-to-action buttons.

## 4. UI Components
*   **Buttons:**
    *   **Primary:** Deep Blue background with White text or Golden background for high-priority actions (e.g., "Book Now").
    *   **Secondary:** Outlined buttons or Light Gray backgrounds.
    *   **Shape:** Slightly rounded corners (border-radius: 4px to 8px) for a modern feel.
*   **Forms:** Clean, labeled input fields with generous padding. Use focus states that highlight fields in the primary blue or gold.
*   **Navigation:** Minimalist header with a clear hierarchy. Mega-menus for complex destination and service categories.

## 5. Design Principles
*   **Sophisticated & Premium:** The use of gold and deep blue conveys a sense of luxury and high-status service.
*   **User-Centric Navigation:** Clear paths for booking, managing flights, and checking in.
*   **Responsive & Performance-Driven:** Fast loading times and a mobile-first approach to cater to modern travelers.
*   **Cultural Identity:** Subtle integration of Kazakh patterns or motifs in backgrounds or accents to reinforce the national brand.
