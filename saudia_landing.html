Proven Capabilities

01

Umrah Booking Platform

Customer Ref: Kanoo Travel

Multi-destination, multi-hotel pilgrimage booking with Haramain Train integration. Supports complex Umrah itineraries spanning Jeddah, Makkah, and Madinah with seamless ground transport connections. Purpose-built for the unique requirements of religious travel.

02

Stopover Programme

Customer Ref: Etihad

Natively integrated into the Amadeus dAPI flight booking flow. Offer stopover packages at the point of ticket purchase — hotels, tours, and transfers surfaced seamlessly within the airline's own booking path. No redirect, no friction.

03

Full End-to-End Holidays Platform

Customer Ref: Malaysia Airlines Holidays

Complete holiday platform: search, dynamic packaging (Flight + Hotel + Tours + Transfers), shopping basket, passenger details, payment, and confirmation. A fully branded Saudia Holidays experience from first search to booking confirmation.

04

B2B Distribution & Agent Hierarchies

Customer Ref: Amadeus + NATAS Singapore

Extensive B2B capabilities with multi-level agent hierarchies and sub-branding. Enable travel agent networks, GSAs, and corporate partners to sell Saudia Holidays under their own brand with configurable markup, commission structures, and reporting.

05

Merchant of Record

Strategic easyGDS Partner

Full-service offering including Merchant of Record through a strategic easyGDS partner. Handle payment processing, refunds, chargebacks, and regulatory compliance — allowing Saudia to offer holidays without the operational overhead of financial intermediation.
